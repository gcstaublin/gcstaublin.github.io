Behind the Scenes

  • Everyone was a Simpsons Character

    Making yourself into a Simpons character has to be some sort of personality test. As we created our own likenesses, we taped them to a wall next the lunch table in the department. I remember sitting and eating lunch staring at these characters, wondering ‘really, is that how you see yourself.’ That might say a little too much about my own personality.
